Can You Paint the Same Day You Drywall?

Every drywall repair services project begins with surface preparation. This includes sanding and priming the drywall. Sanding is necessary to smooth out any bumps or ridges on the drywall surface. Priming helps to seal the drywall and provides a better surface for painting. Once the drywall is prepped, you can begin painting. Be sure to use a paint designed for use on drywall. Drywall repair and painting can be a messy job, so be sure to wear old clothes and protect your floors.
